Exousia - The Dance Club

Exousia is the official dance club of Indian Institute of Technology, Patna. Initiated as a platform to promote dance culture in students, it has bloomed into a full-fledged society of dancers skilled in various dance styles ranging from classical to fusion to western. Exousia caters to a variety of intra and inter college dance competitions including solo, duet, street battles and many other categories.

The club hosts a tasteful series of events namely FlipToe, HeelTurn, Beat It Off, Hookstep, Rhythm and many more. Join the club and feel the joy of Dance!
